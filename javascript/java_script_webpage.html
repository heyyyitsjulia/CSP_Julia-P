Pandas

Habitat

The habitat in which giant pandas live in is mostly dense bamboo forests in the mountainous areas of southwestern china, specifically in provinces like sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu. Since habitat loss is the most serious threat to the panda, establishing new reserves and extending existing ones are crucial to its survival. They have mostly lived in,

  • southern China
  • northern Vietnam
  • northern Myanmar

Survival

To survive, there are many things that the giant panda has to, first of which is eating bamboo, which is 99% of their diet, they spend 14 hours a day eating, so thats their main past time. They have unique wrist bones to grasp and crush bamboo. They also climb trees for protection from other animals or certain weather events/ things that could harm them. In certain areas, their black and white pattern can help the camouglage from other predators.
