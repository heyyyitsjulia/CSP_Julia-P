Tangled

My favorite movie is Tangled, which is the disney version of the rapunzel story. The plot is about a young girl who was kidnapped as a child and is trapped in this tower, thinking that it is her home. One day Flynn Ryder, comes along and finds the tower, she becomes adventurous enough to leave the tower with him to go and see the floating lanterns. She then later finds out that she is the lost princess that they had been looking for all those years. Up in the tower she gets really bored, and in "When will my life begin" she sings about different things she can do. She...

  1. Reads a book
  2. Adds paintings to her gallery
  3. Plays guitar
  4. Bakes
  5. Does pottery
  6. Makes candles
